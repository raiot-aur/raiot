About us

Robotics-Automation and Internet of Things (RAIOT) Lab is introduced by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Amity University Rajasthan to provide the students a platform to showcase their skills in this field. RAIOT lab provide the materials required for the project.
The lab is not just limited to the CSE department. All those who have the skills and wish to persue in the same can come forward and join the team.

The RAIOT Lab is set up with the sincere efforts of Dr. Tarun Kumar Sharma (HOD, CSE Department, AUR).
